Marching Circuits

The following links are for 4 of the largest and most well-known Drum & Bugle Corps and Marching circuits in the United States. Your information provided in the Form section will go to these circuits:



Drum Corps International
  • 4 Divisions: Open Class, World Class, Drumline Battle, and Soundsport
  • Tours start in May, end at World Championships in mid-August; Rehearsal days depend on the corps
  • Ages: 14 to 21 (you can turn 22 during the season)
  • General Fees: anywhere from $500 to $2500 for the season

"To bring the life-enriching benefits and enjoyment of marching music performing arts to more people worldwide. We do this by creating a stage for participating organizations to engage in education, competition, entertainment, and the promotion of individual growth."


Drum Corps Associates
  • Ages: 14 to 100 (no restrictions on upper age)
  • Two Divisions: Class 'A' (35 to 60 members) and Open Class
  • Rehearsals: mainly on weekends
  • Competitions: weekends only, from late June untii World Championships on Labor Day Weekend
  • Fees: from about $500 to $950 for the season

"Drum Corps Associates has provided tens of thousands of individuals with music education and performance opportunities throughout the United States and internationally. As the nation's oldest continuously operating drum corps circuit, DCA corps continue to entertain and excite audiences everywhere."
