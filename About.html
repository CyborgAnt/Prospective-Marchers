About Me

I am a Drum & Bugle Corps marcher, currently with the all-age group Cincinnati Tradition, on the Drum Corps Association (DCA) circuit. 20 years prior, I tried out for the Drum Corps International (DCI) group, the Phantom Regiment, from Rockford, IL. At the time, I only had the money to make a trip to one corps' site to try out.


Today, I wanted to make it simpler for potential marchers to enter their information into a central database, where they could potentially see which groups are looking for which performers, rather than trying to go around the country for tryouts, rather than trying to go around the country for tryouts. This site is the beginning of that database!
