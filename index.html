Welcome!
On this page, people who are looking to try out for a Spring or Summer marching group can enter their info, sections that they are interested in, and any previous and current marching experience, into a database that Directors can view and get in touch with the prospective marcher.

In addition, visitors will find information on some of the most popular marching circuits in the U.S., with links to the various corps and groups inside the linked circuits.

Good luck with your marching endeavors!
