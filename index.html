Welcome! On this page, you will find information and links to 4 of the most well-known Marching circuits in the United States: Drum Corps International (DCI), Drum Corps Associates (DCA), the Small Drum Corps Association (SDCA), and Winter Guard International (WGI).

In addition, you will find a Prospective Marchers Form, where you can send your name, address, and sections that you would like to try out for, to a national database that Corps and Performance Directors can access, speeding up the Audition process.

Good luck with your marching endevors!
